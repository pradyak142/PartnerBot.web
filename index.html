How it works
Up and running in minutes
01

Add the bot

Invite PartnerBot to your server with one click. No complicated setup required.

02

Run /setup

Follow the 5-step wizard to set your partner channel, ad channel, and roles.

03

Post your ad

Features
Everything you need
🔄

Fully Automatic

Runs every 30 minutes without any manual action. Set it up once and let it grow your server.

⚖️

Fair Ping System

Servers within 50 members of each other both get member pings. Bigger size differences get partner pings.

⏳

3 Day Cooldown

Prevents the same two servers pairing repeatedly. Keeps your partner feed fresh and diverse.

✅

Auto Validation

Before every cycle the bot checks all channels are accessible and ads are valid. No silent failures.

📋

Log Channel

Every partnership is logged in your private log channel. Know exactly who you partnered with and when.

⚙️

Easy Config

Update any setting anytime with /config set. No need to redo the full setup wizard.
